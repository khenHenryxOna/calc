Our Story

Passion-Driven
Culinary Artistry

Founded in 2012, Khen's Catering was born from a deep love for Filipino hospitality and world-class cuisine. We believe every celebration deserves food that tells a story — crafted with premium ingredients, served with warmth, and presented with artistry.

Led by Executive Chef Khen Henryx Ona, our team brings over a decade of combined experience from five-star hotels and award-winning restaurants across the Philippines and Asia.
